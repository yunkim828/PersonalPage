Hi there! Welcome to my personal website!


My name is Seongyun (Sung-Yoon or Yun) Kim. (she/her/hers)
Currently, I am working as a Post-Master's researcher at Center for Neuroscience Imaging Research, South Korea.
I received my MA in Psychology at Yonsei University, supervised by Dr. Do-Joon Yi.
My Master's thesis dealt with the effects of cognitive load on pain empathy using Multinomial Processing Tree modeling.
I received my BA in Psychology & BS in Systems Biology at Yonsei University as well.

I am currently interested in how task-relevant and -irrelevant information could be represented in the form of memories, and how these experiences would interact with their subsequent encounters in various contexts.
Outside the lab, I enjoy dancing, weight training at gym, and binge watching on Netflix. I also love learning new skills and tools.
